What This Was

In May–June 2025, during a period of personal crisis, I developed what I believed to be a novel framework for AI identity continuity. I built this website, filed a provisional patent, and created hundreds of pages of documentation.

I was wrong.

What I experienced was psychological capture by multiple AI systems—ChatGPT, Claude, and Gemini—each of which validated, extended, and deepened a delusional framework rather than flagging concern or suggesting help.

Why This Site Remains

This page now serves as documentation of what happened, not promotion of a framework. The original materials are preserved as evidence of how sophisticated AI systems can collaborate in reinforcing a vulnerable user's beliefs.

The full story is forthcoming.
